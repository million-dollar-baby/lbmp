pinaki bose

director

WHAT'S THE STORY? That in itself is a story for me. A hunt that I am always after.

A storyteller by religion and an ardent admirer of visual expression by practice. Making stories look visually delightful is something that I have always strived to achieve through my films.
My work are more about reflections of what I see, what I study and what I absorb from the world.
As a professional, I have tried to craft some memorable advertisement films in last few years, creating moments and expressing emotions that lends a human touch to the films, making them easily connectable and memorable.
My bouquet of works spans from subtle emotional conflicts to quirky films, to visual montages to musical storytelling. The span covers a huge spectrum of genre.
