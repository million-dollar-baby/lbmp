pagarav patel

producer

What makes Pagarav indispensable is his keen insight on creativity and knowledge of logistics. A creative producer who always has a solution. With his vast experience in theatre, he often opens up a new dimension to the thought process of making films, at times throwing new light to ideas. As a professional, works hard, parties harder. No wonder he has executed and produced films for Google, Tata Sky, Coke, Royal Stag, Mc Dowell, Cadbury, along with Dabur, ICICI, Titan, Fair & Lovely, Gillette, UNICEF, Gujrat Maritime Board, Kapasi Handicrafts, Yatri Pan Masala and 150 such more. He has line produced feature films Yahaan, directed by Shoojit Sircar, and Aloo Chat, directed Robby Garewal and has been the executive producer for Aparajita Tumi, directed by Anirudhha Roychowdhury. ;
