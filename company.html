Where the minds clash. Passion and ideas cross path. And films are born. Where we dream, emote, express. And feel happy being recognised. We are in to everything that has got to do with film making with a simple idea, to give every script what it deserves. Be it in creative, production or post production. And in the process make some great films, unprejudiced of medium, budget or value.


PAGARAV PATEL
Co-Founder / Producer



A man who understands film execution at the back of his palm. He might forget the easiest of the names but would never get lost in the most complex structure of numbers. Enough to be a producer? A passionate film maker. After having an extensive career 13 years of executing numerous commercials and feature films at different stages of life and work, he took the plunge to switch on the Light Bulb to create some memorable Motion Pictures.



PINAKI BOSE
Co-Founder / Director



Loves telling stories, observing people and developing characters. A man who understands emotions, dynamics of relationships and humour and how. An extremely finicky visual person. Loves getting in to the details of the narratives. An ardent photographer, lover of music, painting and sports. Having worked as a journalist, a main stream advertising writer he found his niche in making films. He illuminated the Light Bulb with a simple idea to make a platform for meaningful Motion Pictures unbiased of the medium.
